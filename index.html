Core features

A compact, practical stack for field health monitoring.

Real-time Telemetry

Publish sensor readings (BPM, SpO₂, temp, BP) from devices to cloud and view live in the app.

Doctor Dashboard

Doctors can view patient timelines, write prescriptions, and manage appointments.

Offline-first

Local buffering and sync to handle intermittent connectivity in rural areas.

Privacy & Security

Certificate-based device provisioning, secure cloud integration, and per-device policies.

Hardware & Integration

ESP32

Primary microcontroller used on field devices.

Pulse Oximeter (MAX30102)

SpO₂ and heart-rate sensor for non-invasive monitoring.

Temperature (MCP9808)

Accurate digital temperature sensor.

ECG Sensor

Optional ECG front-end for basic waveform capture (prototype stage).

Who benefits

Primary Health Workers

Quick vital scans and patient uploads during village visits.

Doctors

View patient vitals remotely, manage appointments, send prescriptions.

Community

Lower travel burden, faster triage, better continuous care.
